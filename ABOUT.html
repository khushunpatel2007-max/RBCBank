Our History

Founded in 1864, Royal Bank of Canada (RBC) has grown into one of the world’s leading financial institutions. With a strong foundation built on trust, innovation, and integrity, RBC has consistently supported individuals, businesses, and communities across generations.

Today, RBC serves millions of clients globally, delivering modern banking solutions while honoring its rich Canadian heritage.
